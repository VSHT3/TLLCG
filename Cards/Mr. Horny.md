## Ability
Charge: 1 (max 16).
Counter: 5 —> destroy self and damage your hero by 3. 
Whenever this unit or your hero get damaged, double the charges and decrease counter by 1. 
- Once per turn:  
	- Order 2 —> Boost self by 1, then damage a unit by 1.
	- Order 4 —> Boost self by 2, then damage a unit by 1.
	- Order 8 —> Boost self by 2, then damage a unit by 2.

At the end of your turn, counter $\geq$ 3 —> damage a unit by 1.
At the end of your turn, counter $\leq$ 2 —> damage a unit by 2.
At the end of your turn, counter $\leq$ 0 —> destroy self and damage your hero by 3.
