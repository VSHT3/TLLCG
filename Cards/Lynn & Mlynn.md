## Ability
Can not lose more than 2 health for 1 instance of damage.
If you have 3 or more health, Mlynn is active. Otherwise, Lynn is active.
- Mlynn:  
	- At the end of your turn, deal 2 damage to a non-hero unit on a different row, which is not opposite. 
	- Deathblow —> deal 2 damage to an enemy hero.   
- Lynn:  
	- At the end of your, deal 1 damage to a unit on the opposite row. 
	- Deathblow —> draw a card from the neutral deck.
