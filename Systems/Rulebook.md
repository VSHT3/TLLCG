# General stuff
- if there are no units on an enemy baord, a card with “target non-hero unit” may target a hero unit
- if you attempt to play a unit on a full board, it goes to the graveyard instead
## Initialization
1. dice roll for first hero pick
2. each player recieves their shuffled faction deck (stack of cards)
3. shuffle neutral cards and place them face down on the board
4. each player, in the opposite [[Keywords#^order|order]] of first hero pick, draw 5 neutral and 3 faction cards
5. each player gets base 5 **base sellary** per turn
6. player who won 1. starts their turn, then continue in anticlockwise direction
7. the game continues until there is only one hero standing 
8. you can hold up to 10 cards at the end of your turn in your hand
	1. if you have more than 10 cards, discard random cards (by RNG) until you have 10 cards left in your hand 
9. drawing a neutral card costs 3 sallary + 1 per extra draw
10. drawing a faction card costs 4 + 1
11. [[Keywords#^order|order]] of card activation
12. starting from melee row to artillery row, left to right
13. row capacity is 5, 5, 3
## Turn
1. you get sallary
2. cards with *at the start of your turn…* abilities trigger
3. you can play up to two cards from your hand
4. you can discard up to as many cards as you want
5. you can draw as many cards as you want
6. cards with *at the end of your turn…* abilities trigger
7. next player starts their turn
